SBM Petroproducts Pvt Ltd was established in the year 1974. Our work culture rests on the philosophy "TOMORROW'S PROGRESS TODAY". The vision, sheer persistence and far sightedness of the Founder and Group Director, Mr. R. Ragu has enabled SBM group to diversify into various fields of Petroleum Products, Winery, Vineyards and Organic Farming. Cumbum Valley winery and vineyards situated at the foothills of Kodaikanal is the first and only winery in Tamilnadu. It prides itself in growing the economy of the grape growing farmers of the Western Ghats belt and putting Tamil Nadu on the Wine map of India. The vineyards and greenhouse farms are located in the midst of the sprawling 350 acres of land owned and developed by the Group.

SBM is an ISO 9001:2008 certified company with an annual capacity of over two thousand metric tonnes. Goldoyl our flagship brand is manufactured at our exclusive state of the art blending plants at Madhavaram & Kanchipuram. We manufacture a wide range of industrial oils of international standards with guaranteed uninterrupted supplies. Our factories are equipped with the latest quality control laboratories with an in house technical support offering systematic QC checks at every stage of production.

To satisy customer needs and retain leadership by manufacturing and supplying quality products and services through continuous improvement of motivated employees.



MR.R.RAGU , CHAIRMAN & MANAGING DIRECTOR.
